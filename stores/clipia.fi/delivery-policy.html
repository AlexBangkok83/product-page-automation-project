Delivery Issues If you experience delivery issues: - Check tracking information first - Contact the carrier directly for delivery updates - Contact support@clipia.fi if the package is lost or damaged - Report delivery issues within 48 hours of expected delivery ## 9. Address Changes - Address changes must be made before the order ships - Contact support@clipia.fi immediately for address corrections - We cannot guarantee address changes once shipped ## 10. Risk Transfer Risk of loss and title for items pass to the customer upon delivery to the carrier. ## Contact Information For questions about shipping and delivery, contact support@clipia.fi.
